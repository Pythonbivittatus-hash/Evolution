The Premise

Adaptation is not ascent. It is survival under pressure.

You shape a lineage through hostile biomes, choosing mutations as environments tighten, collapse and transform. Every run is a struggle between scarcity, defence, symbiosis and risk.

Evolution is not a march toward perfection. It is what remains after the world has tested every form.

The Loop

A ritual of pressure and response.

  1. I Reveal Pressure

    A biome asserts its demand: cold, hunger, predation, collapse.

  2. II Choose One of Three

    Mutation is opportunity with a cost. You cannot take every path.

  3. III Recalculate Organism

    Traits combine, conflict and awaken synergies inside the lineage.

  4. IV Resolve

    The environment judges the form. Some survive. Some become fossil.

  5. V Adapt

    Carry what endured into the next pressure. The run deepens.
