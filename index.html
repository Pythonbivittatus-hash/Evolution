Digital first. Physical next.

The first version is being developed as an online/digital strategy game, with a physical deck-building version planned alongside it.

The deck

Mutation is not decoration. It is the game.

Evolution is being designed around the drama of biological pressure: scarcity, defence, mobility, awareness, energy and resistance. The aim is a game that feels tactical, readable and atmospheric without revealing too much too early.
